Register for:
La Routa Adventure Race

A maximum of twenty (20) teams will be accepted for the LA ROUTA Adventure Race – ARWS Asian Championship 2025.

Registration is pre-paid and processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Submission of payment constitutes full acceptance of LA ROUTA’s official rules and regulations.

Registration fee inclusions

Option 1: Expanded P35,000 (Philippine Peso)
  • Transportation to and from Tacloban City airport to Race headquarters
  • Two days prior and one day after, accommodation at our partner hotel In, Naval, Biliran Province
  • Full race organization and logistics
  • Race kit for each team, comprising:
    • Official racer identification cards
    • Rulebook
    • Race passport
    • Course map
    • Bike numbers
    • Team race bibs (4), to be worn over uniforms for the duration of the race
    • Gearboxes (3 x 100L)
    • Four (4) Official event shirts
  • Hosted welcome dinner
  • Hosted closing dinner and social gathering
  • Medals, trophies, and cash prizes for winners
Option 2: Basic P25,000 (Philippine Peso)
  • Full race organization and logistics
  • Race kit for each team, comprising:
    • Official racer identification cards
    • Rulebook
    • Race passport
    • Course map
    • Bike numbers
    • Team race bibs (4), to be worn over uniforms for the duration of the race
    • Gearboxes (3 x 100L)
    • Four (4) Official event shirts
  • Hosted welcome dinner
  • Hosted closing dinner and social gathering
  • Medals, trophies, and cash prizes for winners
Option 3: Guests P2,000 (Philippine Peso)
  • Official race id
  • Hosted welcome dinner
  • Hosted closing dinner and social gathering
  • Medals, trophies, and cash prizes for winners
